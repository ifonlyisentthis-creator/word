FAQ

Clear answers, premium support.

Can Afterword read my messages?

No. Encryption happens on-device and keys never leave you. With zero-knowledge mode, even the server cannot decrypt your data key.

What’s the difference between Guardian Vault and Time Capsule?

Guardian Vault uses a single check-in timer for all entries — miss it and everything executes. Time Capsule lets you schedule each entry for a specific date with no check-ins needed.

What is zero-knowledge mode?

A per-entry toggle that keeps the encryption key only on your device. The server stores no copy, so even we cannot decrypt. You manually share the key with your beneficiary.

What if I miss a check-in?

In Guardian Vault mode, the protocol executes. Sent items stay visible for 30 days, then purge. In Time Capsule mode, there is no check-in — entries deliver on their scheduled date.

Can I update recipients?

Yes. Edit any active entry and it re-encrypts on save.

How do beneficiaries access items?

They use view.afterword-app.com with the security key from the email. For zero-knowledge entries, the sender shares the key separately.

How many entries can I create?

Free: 3 entries. Pro: 20 entries. Lifetime: 50 entries. Slots are recovered after sent entries are purged.

Can I switch between modes?

Yes, in Account Settings. You must clear all active entries before switching modes.
